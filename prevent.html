HOW TO PREVENT DEPRESSION?


In fact, it is not yet known for certain what effective way to prevent depression. However, several preventive measures that can be done are as follows:
  1. Manage and overcome stress as best as possible, such as doing yoga, meditation, or activities that you enjoy regularly. Talking to someone close to you or a therapist can also help.
  2. Arrange sufficient rest and sleep time.
    As much as possible, avoid staying up late or sleeping late, with a minimum of 8 hours of quality sleep per day.
  3. Avoid consuming alcoholic drinks.
    Alcohol can lower serotonin and norepinephrine levels, which help regulate mood . Lower levels of the chemical can make people who are depressed even more depressed. Alcohol temporarily reduces the effects of stress hormones. This can worsen your depression symptoms because it puts pressure on the brain and nervous system.
  4. Adopt a healthy lifestyle, such as eating healthy food with balanced nutrition, exercising regularly, and so on.
  5. Limit useless use of social media.
    Spending too much time on social media can lead to feelings of lack of support and pressure to constantly compare yourself to others. Therefore, limit the use of social media that is not useful.
  6. Don't isolate yourself. When you feel depressed or sad, don't isolate yourself.
    Try to stay connected with others and talk about feelings. Isolating yourself can actually make your thoughts more negative.
  7. Look for positive and enjoyable hobbies and activities Find activities that you like, such as reading books, painting, gardening, or playing music.
    Hobbies can provide feelings of happiness and strengthen a sense of accomplishment.
  8. Treat health problems suffered regularly.
    If you have difficulty dealing with your feelings or feel depressed, don't hesitate to seek help from a doctor or therapist. They can help you get strategies for facing challenges in life.
  9. Consult a psychologist or psychiatrist if you feel deep and prolonged sadness.
