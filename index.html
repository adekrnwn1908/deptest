DEPTEST

Find out your depression level using The Patient Health Questionnaire-9 is a psychometric instrument that was originally part of the Primary Care Evaluation of Mental Disorders (PRIME-MD) aimed at screening for mental disorders in general. Later in 2001, the questionnaire was developed separately by Spitzer et al from Columbia University, New York, USA, to specifically identify depression

How to prevent depression?

This project aims to develop a web platform that can help young people identify their depression levels early on. This online depression test will be a useful tool in the effort to prevent mental health problems.

Trick or Treat

Living with mental illness is not easy, but treatment can help improve the sufferer's quality of life. Try seeing a medical professional to ask for help with treatment to get better. If it is still relatively mild, self-care may help. If self-treatment is no longer effective, the sufferer may need psychiatric counseling or prescription medication from a doctor.
